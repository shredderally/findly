Operated by Northbound Holdings. Last updated June 2026.

1. What Findly is

Findly is a directory connecting customers in Ghana with identity-verified local service providers. Browsing listings is free. Viewing a provider's direct contact information requires a one-time unlock fee. Providers pay a recurring listing fee to remain visible in search.

2. Provider verification

Providers submit a government-issued ID and a liveness selfie during signup. Verification confirms identity only — it is not a guarantee of work quality, licensing, or insurance. Customers should still use reasonable judgment before engaging a provider.

3. The provider pledge

Providers agree at signup that any verified report of scamming, fraud, or misrepresentation results in immediate, permanent removal of their listing and permanent blacklisting of their phone number from the platform. Serious cases may be reported to local authorities.

4. Payments

All payments are processed through Paystack. Unlock fees and listing fees are non-refundable once a contact has been revealed or a listing has gone live, except where required by law or at Findly's discretion in cases of demonstrated platform error.

5. No liability for third-party conduct

Findly is a directory, not a party to any agreement between a customer and a provider. Findly is not responsible for the quality, safety, or legality of services rendered by listed providers.

6. Account suspension

Findly may suspend or remove any account at its discretion, including for fraud, abuse, fake listings, or violation of these terms.
